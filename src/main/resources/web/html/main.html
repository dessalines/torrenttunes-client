Install TorrentTunes

If you'd like to share music on your computer, and help upload your library to the bittorrent network, install the TorrentTunes client.

Download TorrentTunes

Requires:
  • Java 8
  • Firewalls turned off(to allow torrenting)

Do not share your music unless you plan to have this client running all day. If you close TorrentTunes, you will no longer be sharing your songs.

I will remove your songs from the server if you do not continue to run the client in order to share them with others.

If you find that your songs have been removed from the server, click Settings -> Clear local Database, and reshare your songs.
